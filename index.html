Xiaolu Xiong


My research designs intelligent tutoring systems that help to improve students' robust learning level and long-term retention performance. I also build machine learning models to understand learning and memory. By combining these two parts, My work has the ability to provide the highly efficient ways to help students and teachers while maintains great interpretability for my users. Since 2012, there are over 20,000 students used the products of my work.

My advisors are Professor Joseph Beck and Professor Neil Heffernan.
